Lemon Cake recipe

A simple lemon cake to eat as dessert after a well deserved meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch square baking pan.
  2. Beat sugar and butter together in a mixing bowl using an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract until well combined.
